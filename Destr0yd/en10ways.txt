									~ 10 ways to reset the register ~

It is often necessary to reset the register. Maybe, here you will find new, more "universal" way than just to xor register. :)
Just want to warn you that I'm studying assembly, I don't know all the commands. I'm not a god.

1. Classic
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov	ax, 09h
|	xor	ax, ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. Register minus Register
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
|	sub ax, ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. Push the empty register into the stack, and then restore it
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	push ax			; must be zero
|	mov ax, 09h
|	pop ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. Multiply the register by zero
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
|	mul cx
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. The first thing that comes to the head beginner
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
|	mov ax, 00h
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. We exchange the register for empty
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
|	xchg cx, ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. Shift right to yourself
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cl, 09h
|	shr cl, cl
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. Shift left to yourself
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cl, 09h
|	shl cl, cl
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. Reset the counter register
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cx, 09h
|	loop $
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

10. Reset the register with a loop
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cx, 09h
|	mov ax, cx
|	loop $
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
