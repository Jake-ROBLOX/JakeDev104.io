Types of viruses

When virus infects other program(PE), this is a file-infecting virus. Also viruses can infect MSOffice files, MBR. 
Viruses that infect MSOffice files are called macroviruses because it uses macro's. Viruses that infect MBR
(Master Boot Record) or boot record of floppy are called boot viruses.

HLL viruses

These viruses are written only on high level languages such as C++, C, Pascal etc.

HLLP (High Level Language Parasitic) - are viruses that infect without any damage to the original program, 
these use a traditional method of infection.

HLLC (High Level Language Companion) - are viruses that use other methods of file-infection.

HLLO (High Level Language Overwriting) - are the most simple viruses, these just replace the original program code with it's own.
