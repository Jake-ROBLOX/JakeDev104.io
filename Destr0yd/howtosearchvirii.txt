									~ How to search for viruses? ~

Searching for viruses isn't easy. And I don't mean downloading everything from unknown sites, because you'll be hard-pressed to find anything useful.
That won't work. Especially if you're not a moron, you won't even try googling "download a virus."
"Download a virus without SMS or registration"...

Method number 1:

You can try searching for the malware's source code on a source code site (there are plenty of them), that is,
use keywords such as "virus source", "trojan code" or even just "virus", "trojan".
Thus, having found, for example, another Trojan in C++, you will compile it.

Method number 2:

If you have at least one website about virology or something similar, then it usually contains viruses, Trojans,
their source codes. In addition, you may find links to other similar sites where, accordingly,
You will find the same thing.

Good luck.
