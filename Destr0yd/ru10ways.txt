									~ 10 ways to reset a register ~

Clearing the registry is often necessary. Perhaps you'll find a new, more universal method here than simply Xeroxing.
register. :) I want to warn you right away that I'm studying assembler, I'm not familiar with all the instructions, and I'm not a god.

1. Classic
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov	ax, 09h
|	xor	ax, ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. Register minus Register
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
|	sub ax, ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. Push an empty register onto the stack, then restore it.
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| push ax ; must be zero
|	mov ax, 09h
|	pop ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. Multiply the register by zero
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
| thank you
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. The first thing that comes to mind for a beginner
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
|	mov ax, 00h
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. Change the register to empty
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov ax, 09h
|	xchg cx, ax
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. Shift right onto yourself
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cl, 09h
|	shr cl, cl
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. Shift left onto yourself
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cl, 09h
|	shl cl, cl
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. Reset the counter register
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cx, 09h
|	loop $
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

10. Clear the register using a loop
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|	mov cx, 09h
|	mov ax, cx
|	loop $
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
